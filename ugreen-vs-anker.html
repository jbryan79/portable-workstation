What 2 Years of Testing Taught Me

1. Anker's Brand Premium Isn't Always Worth It

I wanted to love Anker. They're the "safe choice" everyone recommends. But after three hub failures in two years, I couldn't justify the 50% price premium anymore. Their marketing is excellent, but UGREEN's engineering quietly beats them in durability.

2. Heat Management Makes or Breaks USB-C Hubs

Every single Anker hub I owned got uncomfortably hot during extended use. The plastic construction traps heat, which degrades internal components over time. UGREEN's aluminum shells aren't just for looks - they're functional heat sinks that extend product lifespan dramatically.

3. Both Brands Are Honest (Unlike Generic Junk)

Compared to $15 no-name Amazon hubs, both UGREEN and Anker are leagues ahead. Their specs are accurate, their build quality is real, and warranties actually mean something. The debate isn't "good vs bad" - it's "great vs excellent."
